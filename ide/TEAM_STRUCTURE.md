## Decision Making

### Process

1. **RFC Draft** - Author writes proposal
2. **Review Period** - 3 days for feedback
3. **Discussion** - Address concerns
4. **Decision** - Platform Lead + relevant leads
5. **Document** - Update ADRs

### Escalation

- **Technical disputes** → Platform Lead
- **Scope changes** → Product Manager
- **Resource conflicts** → Project Sponsor

## Team Values

1. **User First** - Every decision considers user impact
2. **Quality Over Speed** - Better to delay than ship broken
3. **Transparent** - Blockers surfaced immediately
4. **Collaborative** - Cross-IDE learnings shared
5. **Pragmatic** - Simple solutions preferred
